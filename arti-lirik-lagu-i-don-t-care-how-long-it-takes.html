7. It takes 2 hours for Sally to clean room while it takes 4 hours for Jean to clean room. if they work together, how long they would need?​


Jawaban:

1 jam 20 menit

Penjelasan:

[tex]time = \frac{sally \times jean}{sally + jean} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{2 \times 4}{2 + 4} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{8}{6} = \frac{4}{3} jam[/tex]

[tex] \frac{4}{3} jam \times 60 = 80menit[/tex]

80 menit = 1 jam 20 menit

16. if it takes micah 4 minutes to swim 1/10 of kilometers how long would it take him to swim 2 kilometers​


4 × 2 ÷ 1/10

= 8 × 1/10

= 8 × 10

= 80 minutes

Jawab:

it'd take him 80 minutes.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

4 minutes to swim 1/10 kilometres

40 minutes to swim 1 kilometres

2 kilometres is a double of 1 kilometre so, it would take the double of the time.

Thus, micah would take 80 minutes to swim 2 kilometres long.
