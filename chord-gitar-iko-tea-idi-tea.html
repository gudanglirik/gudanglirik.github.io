26. hasan membeli 2 cup bubble tea dan 1 cup thai tea seharga Rp 50.000 jika harga 1 cup Thai tea 15.000 berapakah harga 1 cup bubble tea


Jawaban:

17500

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

cara cepat =(50.000-15000):2

=17500

misal: 1 cup bubble tea=x

1 cup thai tea. = y

maka

2x+y = 50.000

y = 15.000

maka

2x+15000=50000

2x = 50000-15000

x = 35000:2

x. =17500

misal: 1 cup bubble tea: x
1 cup thai tea:y

diket: y:15.000

ditanya: 2x+y=50.000
x?

jawab: 2x+y=50.000
2x+15.000=50.000
2x=50.000-15.000
2x=35.000

[tex]x = \frac{35.000}{2} = 17.500[/tex]
Jadi 1 cup bubble tea adalah 17.500

0,02 × 1000 = 20 liter

Sehingga:

20 - (9 × 1,8) = 20 - 16,2 = 3,8 liter

Jadi, sisanya 3,8 liter.

40. Soal: Is she drinking tea? The passive form is ... Is tea being drunk by her? Is tea being drunk by she? Is tea being drink by she? Is tea being drink by her? Tolong bantu ya


Jawaban:

Is tea being drunk by her?

Penjelasan:

Karena saat membuat kalimat pasif kita menggunakan verb 2.

Verb 1 > Verb 2

Drink > Drunk

Penggunaan subject "she" biasanya digunakan untuk kalimat aktif  karena kata "she" harus didepan kalimat sedangkan penggunaan subject 'her" digunakan untuk kalimat pasif.
